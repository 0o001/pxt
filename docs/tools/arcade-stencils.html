MakeCode Arcade Stencils

This tool takes a MakeCode Arcade image and turns into a set of pixelated stencils. Cut out the senticals and use them to paint your image!

Copy the source of a MakeCode Arcade image into the textbox below and click generate to turn it into DXF stencils.

Use a laser cutter, Cricut or hobby stencil to cut out the stencils.

Paint each layer with color markers or spray paints, you can also paint t-shirts.
